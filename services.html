OUR SERVICES

SALES OF MUSICAL EQUIPMENTS

Here at jeremyMusicWorld we provide sales of musical instruments to the community. We separate ourselves from our direct competitors by paying intense personal attention to our customers, and educating our clients about the products they need or desire before, during, and after the sale. We offer creative programs to reward customer loyalty, and we provide on-site repair services for the items we sell. We consider our staff our partners and insist they prosper equitably with the growth and success of the company. We will have the largest selection of School Band and Orchestral instruments in the city of Port Harcourt, Establish a well-respected music lesson department and start a rapport early with school band and church music leaders. and will use that advantage to separate ourselves from the competition.

MIXING AND MASTERING

We help artists record their songs, album, or project. And bring the ideas of the artist to reality. We make sure that the finished product is as good as it can be and that it meets the vision of the artist (as has been contractually laid out). While there’s no 100% sure way of making sure a song is a hit, we make sure using available alogarithm that it has the pontential to be a hit.Our producers have an encyclopedic knowledge of music as well as the technical expertise of the gear and they’re up to the minute on today’s formats, genres, sounds, production techniques etc.
